About SakshiEvents

Crafting unforgettable moments since 2013

Our Story

Founded in 2013, SakshiEvents emerged from a passion for creating extraordinary experiences that leave lasting impressions. What began as a small boutique event planning service has evolved into one of the most sought-after luxury event management companies.

We believe that every celebration tells a unique story, and our mission is to bring those stories to life with meticulous attention to detail, creative innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Over the years, we've had the privilege of orchestrating over 850 events, from intimate garden weddings to grand corporate galas, each one a testament to our dedication to turning visions into reality.

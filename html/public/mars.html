Mars

Overview

Mars, the red planet, has a thin atmosphere and the largest volcano in the Solar System (Olympus Mons). It's a prime target for exploration and future human missions.

Key facts

  • Mean radius: 3,389.5 km
  • Mass: 6.42 × 10^23 kg
  • Orbital period: ~687 Earth days
  • Thin CO₂ atmosphere; evidence of past liquid water

Sources: NASA – Mars

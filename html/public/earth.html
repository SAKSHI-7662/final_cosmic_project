Earth

Overview

Earth is the only known planet to harbor life. It has a nitrogen-oxygen atmosphere, abundant liquid water, and a magnetic field that helps shield the surface from solar wind.

Key facts

  • Mean radius: 6,371 km
  • Mass: 5.97 × 10^24 kg
  • Orbital period: ~365.25 days
  • Surface: ~71% water; breathable atmosphere for known life

Sources: NASA – Earth
